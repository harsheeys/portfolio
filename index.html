Stock Market Database and Analysis

Our project delves into the intricate world of stock market analysis, aiming to decode the complexities of this dynamic financial arena. We've harnessed a diverse dataset encompassing stock information, transactions, and user profiles to uncover valuable insights. By employing a blend of technical indicators and a probabilistic Hidden Markov Model, we sought to predict stock trends and minimize trading risks. Our journey illuminated the power of NoSQL tools like Couchbase for handling versatile data structures, while SQL tools offered solid foundations for conventional data management. With a keen eye on future enhancements, we look forward to expanding our database, incorporating more stock exchanges, and refining user experience with additional functionalities. This project underscores the critical role of data analysis in financial decision-making, empowering investors to navigate the ever-evolving stock market landscape.

Brain Tumor Detection

I spearheaded a project designed to assist medical professionals in the accurate detection of brain tumors. This was achieved through a dedicated platform where MRI brain scans could be uploaded for analysis. Leveraging the power of the KNN algorithm, we refined image segmentation processes. Subsequently, these segmented images were trained using a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model. With a robust dataset of around 2,060 MRI images, our model attained an impressive accuracy of 98.1%, marking a significant stride in diagnostic capabilities for neurology professionals.It emphasizes the professional and technical aspects of the project and highlights its significance in the medical field.
